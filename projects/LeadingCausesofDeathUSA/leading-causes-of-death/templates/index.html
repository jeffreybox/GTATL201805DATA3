STEP 2: STAY INFORMED - Utilize frequently updated resources and reports made available to the public. By reading reports on health statistics, you can become aware of changes in population health as well as updates in medical innovation. US News & Health is one of many resources available for this information. Stay informed on statistics and changes in Healthcare! Visit US News & Health Report for more information.

STEP 3: USE RESOURCES - Preventative health is often overlooked in a our society focused on reactive medicine. However, proactivity is becoming more prevalent as the public is becoming more aware and the reimbursement/payment models are shifting. Use studies provided by organizations like the CDC to find out more about what you can do to reduce risk factors - especially in the ten areas reported on in this analysis!
